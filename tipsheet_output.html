How to Read This Sheet — Glossary
TermWhat it means
Model %What our stats model estimates the true probability to be, based on season win rates, points margins, recent form, and home advantage.
Implied %What the bookmaker's odds imply the probability is. Calculated as 1 ÷ decimal odds. e.g. $4.00 odds = 25% implied probability.
EdgeThe gap between our model and the bookie. Positive = value bet (bookie has underpriced it). e.g. +12% means our model gives it 12% more chance than the bookie does.
✅ Green tickPositive edge — our model thinks the bookie's odds are too generous. Potential value bet.
❌ Red crossNegative edge — the bookie has overpriced this. Generally avoid.
STRONG VALUEEdge greater than +10%. The bookie looks significantly off — strong value bet.
VALUEEdge between +3% and +10%. A solid value bet.
MARGINALEdge under +3%. Slight positive edge but close to breakeven. Use with caution.
Success RateOur model's estimated probability of this bet winning. e.g. 62% = wins roughly 6 times out of 10 long-term. This is NOT a guarantee.
Exp. Return / $10Average $ back per $10 staked if the model is correct long-term. e.g. $14.20 = $4.20 average profit per $10. Does not mean you will win every bet.
Try Rate / gameHow many tries this player scores per game on average this season. e.g. 0.62 = scores a try in ~62% of games.
ATS — Anytime Try ScorerBet wins if the player scores a try at any point in the match.
FTS — First Try ScorerBet wins only if the player scores the very first try of the match. Higher odds, lower probability — FTS model weights towards positions that typically score first (Fullbacks, Wingers).
Line / HandicapA points head start or deficit. e.g. Brisbane -7.5 = Brisbane must win by 8+ points. +7.5 = team can lose by up to 7 and still win the bet.
Safety MultiLegs chosen for highest win probability. More likely to land — lower combined odds.
Value MultiLegs chosen for highest edge — where bookies look most wrong. Higher odds but less likely to all land.
Best of Week MultiTop recommended pick from each game combined. Balances edge and probability.
Combined OddsAll leg odds multiplied together. e.g. $2.00 × $3.50 = $7.00 combined.
Combined ProbAll model probabilities multiplied together. The realistic chance of the whole multi landing. e.g. 60% × 45% = 27%.
